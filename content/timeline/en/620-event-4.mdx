## The Severity of Illness and the Prophet's ﷺ Invitation

After the illness of **Abu Talib, the Prophet's ﷺ uncle**, became severe – he who had taken care of him as a child, supported him as an adult, aided him in his call, and protected him from the harms of the polytheists – the Prophet ﷺ entered upon him. Abu Jahl was also present. The Prophet ﷺ said:
> **"O my uncle, say: 'There is no god but Allah,' a word by which I will argue for you before Allah."**

Abu Jahl and Abdullah ibn Abi Umayyah then said: **"O Abu Talib, do you turn away from the religion of Abdul-Muttalib?"**
They did not cease speaking to him until the last thing he said to them was: **"Upon the religion of Abdul-Muttalib."**

## Abu Talib: A Protector for the Prophet ﷺ

Abu Talib had been **the fortress that protected the Prophet ﷺ from the attacks of the dignitaries and the foolish**, but he remained upon the religion of his forefathers, and thus did not succeed.

**Details of the Event:**

In the month of Rajab, 2 AH, the Messenger of Allah ﷺ dispatched a military detachment that included **Sa’d ibn Abi Waqqas** (may Allah be pleased with him) to a region in the Hijaz known as **Rabigh**.

**Key Highlights:**
* **First Combat:** This encounter is historically distinguished as the *first instance of actual combat* in the history of Islam.
* **The First Archer:** During this skirmish, the polytheists closed in on the Muslims. In response, Sa’d ibn Abi Waqqas defended the group using his bow and arrows.

> "Sa’d ibn Abi Waqqas (may Allah be pleased with him) earned the honor of being the **first person to shoot an arrow** in the cause of Allah."

This event demonstrated the courage of the Sahaba when facing opposition and marked a pivotal moment in the early defensive operations of the Muslim community.

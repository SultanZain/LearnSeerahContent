**Details of the Event:**

In the month of **Sha'ban**, in the second year of the Hijrah, Allah the Almighty ordained the fasting of the month of **Ramadan** as a mandatory pillar of Islam for the Muslims.

**Timing and Context:**
Historians and scholars of *Sirah* (Prophetic biography) note that this obligation was revealed shortly before the **Battle of Badr**. Prior to this, Muslims used to fast on the day of *Ashura* and three days of every month, but this was superseded by the month-long obligation.

**The Divine Decree:**
The command was established through the revelation of the verses in Surah al-Baqarah:

The obligation of fasting served as a spiritual preparation for the believers, strengthening their discipline, patience, and devotion to Allah just as they were beginning to face significant trials and major battles.
